Ashwagandha (Withania somnifera)

Ashwagandha is also called 'Indian Ginseng'. It is a powerful adaptogen that reduces stress, anxiety, and fatigue.

It strengthens the immune system, increases stamina, and improves sleep. In Ayurveda, it is considered a rejuvenator (Rasayana).

Brahmi (Bacopa monnieri)

Brahmi is a memory enhancer plant. It activates brain neurons and improves concentration, learning, and memory power.

Brahmi is called a "middle rasayana" because it supports the nervous system and reduces stress.

Amla (Emblica officinalis)

Amla is a superfood rich in vitamin C. It is renowned for its immunity boost, skin glow, and hair health.

In Ayurveda, it is called a tridosha balancing fruit which detoxifies the liver and improves digestion.

Tulsi (Ocimum sanctum)

Tulsi, also known as holy basil, is considered the "Queen of Herbs" in Ayurveda. It is excellent for respiratory health.

Tulsi has antibacterial, antiviral, and antifungal properties and naturally boosts immunity.

Shatavari (Asparagus racemosus)

Shatavari is a manly female health tonic. It balances hormones, aids fertility and lactation.

It is a natural cooling herb that balances bile, supports digestion and improves overall stamina.

Giloy (Tinospora cordifolia)

Giloy is called "Amrita" in Ayurveda, which means "nectar of immortality." It is a fever reducer, immunity booster, and detoxifier.

It is beneficial for diseases like malaria, dengue, and typhoid. Giloy also purifies the liver and blood.
